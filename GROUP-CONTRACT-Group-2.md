# Group Contract - Group 2
## Group Allocation
Grades will by default be allocated equally among members. In cases where a member submits an extremely low amount of work, their grading may be adjusted to give other members more.
## Communication
Main form of communication will be done through the Teams channel in order to account for differing schedules. If is non-responsive through teams, email will be the next line of communication. If email is not responded to in a timely manner, Collaborative Learning Facilliator(CLF) will be contacted about the situation
## Task Ownership
Tasks will be assigned with work distributed as equally as possible and will have an opportunity for members to discuss which tasks they prefer. If a member is or knows they will be falling behind on work, they must contact the group regardless of reasoning. They will then need to discuss with the group how and when they will catch up or if they will require assistance to finish the task. Failure to notify unfinished work will be brought up with CLF.
## Diverse Working Styles
The group acknowledges and respects that members have different working styles, schedules, and strengths. We will accommodate these differences by setting clear, flexible internal deadlines prior to the final submission date. Members are encouraged to communicate their preferred working methods early so tasks can be assigned that align with individual strengths while ensuring the project progresses smoothly for everyone.
